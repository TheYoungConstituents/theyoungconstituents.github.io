Unmuted

Meet Unmuted: The Operational Framework

Unmuted is the direct result of a collaboration between two youth-led organizations: TYC and the IYCGE. The core mission of Unmuted is to capture and highlight youth voices across Canada, transforming raw opinions, experiences, and struggles into actionable, high-visibility media.
